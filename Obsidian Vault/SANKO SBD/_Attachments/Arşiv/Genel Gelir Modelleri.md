

How to create revenue via assets (Decoupled from your time):

You can do business with existing (Buy) or original (Build) instruments
If it is for example digital i.e. if your only input is your mind and time it is a Create
You can generate revenue by either capital gains (buy+sell), passive gains (buy a cash generating asset) or by doing both.
It may be existing or to be built

- Buy an appreciating asset and sell it (==Trading==)
	- Non-dividend stocks, crypto, commodities buy + sell
	- Real estate no rent + sell
- Buy a cash generating asset and hold (==M&A==)
	- Dividend stocks, funds buy + hold
	- Real estate with rent
	- Buying operating businesses or projects
- Buy a cash generating asset and sell (==Parking==)
	- Dividend Stocks, funds buy + sell
	- Real estate with rent + sell
- Build a cash generating asset and sell (==Entrepreneurship==)
	- A physical business run + exit
	- Intellectual Property license + sell
- Build a cash generating asset and hold (==Cashflow Engine==)
	- A physical business run
	- A digital business
	- A digital product (Content, ebook etc.)
	- Intellectual Property licensing (Patents, software, creative content)
	- Automated E-commerce (Dropshipping, Print on Demand)
- Build an asset and sell it (==Productization==)
	- IPO, ICO
	- IP selling
	- Zanaatler
- Sell a no longer lucrative operation (==Exit==) - is due to necessity, not pre-planned
