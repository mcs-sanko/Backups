5 Commandments for building a business
	Commandment of Need: People has to need the thing you are offering, it should be a valid problem.
	Commandment of Entry: Ideally build a business with a high barrier to entry so that less competition and imitators can generate.
	Commandment of Control: At every step of the way be able to control as many things as possible. Youtube for example is entirely dependant on watchers and you are not in ccontrol. Ideally you should be able to dig yourself out of the grave if necessary.
	Commandmennt of Scale: Build a business that has a potential to scale up. Law of effection: To make millions you need to affect million.
	Commandment of Time: True wealth is where you don't have to show up to make money.
