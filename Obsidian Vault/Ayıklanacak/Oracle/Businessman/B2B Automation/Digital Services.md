# Non Custom Ideas
1.  **Template-Based Website Development:** Create a library of customizable website templates tailored to various industries. Charge businesses an upfront fee for the template and offer ongoing maintenance and support through a retainer model.
    
2.  **Content Subscription Service:** Develop a platform that offers a library of high-quality content (e.g., articles, images, videos) relevant to different niches. Businesses can pay an upfront fee for access to the content and a monthly retainer for updates and new additions.
    
3.  **Social Media Content Scheduler:** Build a tool that allows businesses to schedule and automate their social media posts across multiple platforms. Charge an initial setup fee and offer ongoing support and updates through a retainer.
    
4.  **Inventory Management System:** Create an inventory management system that can be tailored to different types of businesses (e.g., retail, e-commerce, manufacturing). Charge an upfront setup fee and provide ongoing support through a retainer.
    
5.  **Employee Training Platform:** Develop a modular employee training platform that can be customized for various industries. Charge an initial setup fee based on the modules selected, with an ongoing retainer for access, updates, and support.
    
6.  **Subscription Billing Software:** Offer a subscription billing solution that can be used by subscription-based businesses across different sectors. Charge an upfront setup fee and provide ongoing support and maintenance through a retainer model.
    
7.  **Customer Feedback and Survey Platform:** Create a feedback and survey platform that businesses can use to gather customer insights. Charge an upfront fee for setup and customization, with a retainer for ongoing support and data analysis.
    
8.  **Marketing Campaign Dashboard:** Develop a marketing campaign dashboard that can be configured for different marketing needs. Charge an initial setup fee and provide ongoing support and data analysis through a retainer.
    
9.  **Sales Funnel Automation Tool:** Build a sales funnel automation tool that businesses can customize for their specific products or services. Charge an upfront fee for setup and provide ongoing support through a retainer.
    
10.  **E-commerce Integration Service:** Offer a service that integrates e-commerce platforms (e.g., Shopify, WooCommerce) with other business systems. Charge an upfront fee for integration and provide ongoing support through a retainer.
