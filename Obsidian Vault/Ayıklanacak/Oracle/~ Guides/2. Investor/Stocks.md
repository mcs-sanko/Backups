Sectors (GICS)
- **Consumer Discretionary (CONS DISC)**: Companies that produce or sell non-essential goods and services. Examples include retailers, media, and consumer services.
- **Consumer Staples (CONS STPL)**: Companies that produce or sell essential goods that are necessary for daily living. Examples include food, beverages, and household products.
- **Energy**: Companies involved in the production and distribution of energy, such as oil, gas, and renewable energy.
- **Financials**: Companies offering financial services, including banks, insurance companies, and investment firms.
- **Health Care (HEALTH)**: Companies providing medical services, creating medical equipment, or producing pharmaceuticals and biotechnology products.
- **Industrials**: Companies involved in manufacturing and distributing goods used in construction, engineering, and other industrial applications.
- **Materials**: Companies that produce or supply raw materials, including metals, chemicals, and paper.
- **Real Estate**: Companies involved in the development, management, and investment in real estate properties.
- **Technology**: Companies involved in the development and distribution of technology products and services, including software, hardware, and IT services.
- **Communication Services (COMMUNICATION SVS)**: Companies providing communication services, including telecom services, media, and entertainment.
- **Utilities**: Companies providing essential services such as electricity, water, and natural gas.
