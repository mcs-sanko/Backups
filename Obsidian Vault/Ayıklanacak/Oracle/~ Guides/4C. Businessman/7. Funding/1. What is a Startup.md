- After finalizing your business plan this means that you now have a problem, a solution and a strategy. And if you did the previous steps correctly each of these are sound as well. The next step is to build it, this will take time, effort and money. Time and effort have to be spent regardless but if you can’t afford to finance yourself this is the first step you may be considering external funding. (i.e. seed (Since you are not operational yet) funding)
- After the entrepreneur gathers their team and after what the problem and the initial solution is going to be is decided, now is the time to build that solution. Building, testing and scaling your solution takes time. In this step of the process you will be building a startup, receive funding to keep you and your people working and start selling your product.
