- How might we?
	- Çözümden başlamıyosun önce soruyu farklı şekillerde baştan tanımlıyorsun, soruda zenginlik yaratıyorsun:
	- Önce soruyu genişlet, sonra içgörü ile gözlem ile vs. daralt. Daralttığından geri genişlet, geri daralt etc.
	- ![](Aspose.Words.04f7dd6b-5f2b-4c01-aea1-460e753bf61d.011.png)
	- Problemleri bulduk.
	- Problemleri gruplara ayır, her grubun unique bir başlığı olacak
	- Her grubun sorununu bir how might we …? Şeklinde yaz.
	- ![](Aspose.Words.04f7dd6b-5f2b-4c01-aea1-460e753bf61d.012.png)
	- Bu how might we işte senin problem yazımın.
	- Chosen HMW: How might we better equip our students to get good internships and jobs?
		- We were surprised to notice \_\_\_ (interesting observations/quotes from the interview)\_\_\_\_ Unanimous olması.
		- We wonder if this means \_\_\_\_(inference about the observation)\_\_\_
		- How might we.....? (question based on the inference above)ve bunu da Jamboard içerisinde belirtiyoruz.

	- Empathy Map
		- Who is the person we want to understand?
		- Someone looking for a first job or internship.
	- What is the situation they are in?
		- They need to find an internship to graduate.
		- They need to find a job for after graduation.
		- They are under constant pressure to balance other things in their lives with the job hunt.
		- They are under pressure from their parents to find a job/internship.
		- They keep hearing about jobs and internships their friends have found.
	- What is their role in the situation?
		- They are the candidate in a sea of candidates.
	- What do they need to do differently?
		- They need to outshine the competition.
		- Their CVs need to match job offerings constantly with keywords.
		- Their experience must be relevant all the time.
		- They must have showable past work in the field.
	- What jobs do they want or need to get done?
		- They need to keep up with their classes
		- They want to get into their applications
		- They want to have an easy selection process
	- How will we know they were successful?
		- They get the intenship/job they wanted.
	- What to they see in the marketplace?
		- General application platforms: Staj seferbirliği, vizyonergenç
		- Specific application platforms: www.company.com/careers
	- What do they see in their immediate environment?
		- People who are getting jobs
		- People who are getting internships
		- People who don’t care if they get a job/internship right away
	- What do they see others saying and doing?
		- Since it is a competitive environment, they are probably not seeing anything.
	- What are they watching and reading?
	- What have we heard them say?
		- They are stressed
		- Çok iyi X üniversitesinde okuyorum, iş bulmak benim için de bu kadar zor olmamalı.
	- What can we imagine them saying?
		- Keşke tek bir tane başvuru sistemi olsaydı.
		- Çok stresli süreç, her aşama arasında çok fazla bekleniyor.
		- Sonuçların açıklanma süreleri arasında çok fark var, hangisini kabul ya da reddedeceğini bilemiyor.
	- What do they do today?
		- Classes, studying, chilling, repeating.
		- What behavior have we observed?
		- Very anxious, stressed about their future
		- Annoyed that their school education isn’t enough to get a position
	- What can we imagine them doing?
		- Getting paralyzed under the workload
		- Applying to tens of positions constantly
		- Constantly revising their CV, application letter, documents.
		- What are they hearing others say?
		- I got a job
		- I am applying to here
	- What are they hearing from friends?
	- What are they hearing from colleagues?
	- What are they hearing second-hand?
	- What are their fears, frustrations and anxieties?
	- What are their wants, needs, hopes, dreams?
