- THE RISK ASSOCIATED TO ALLIANCE FORMATION
- Misappropriation risks
	- Big company learns the startups secrets and absorbs it sselfishly
	- Large companies can provide a lot of assets or easily absorb (steal) their secrets
- Don’t swim with sharks.
	- Different markets and different technological knowledge based companies are less likely to absorb you.
- Defense mechanisms
	- Legal protections
		- Patents
		- Startup’s knowledge is not always patentable
	- Time protection
	- Social protection
		- Network reputation
	- Contracts
		- More articulate, higher negotiation cost
		- NDA, NCA etc.
	- Scope of alliance
		- Limit the number and type of activities to protect your secrets
	- Hold up hazards
		- Renegotiation may occur due to companies request
	- Equaity alliance
	- Choose trusted partners
	- Partners don’t put enough effort into the startup
		- Large companies form many alliances to distribute risk. And focus on the working alliances only.
	- Risk mitigation strategy
		- Equity alliance
		- Alliance contract
