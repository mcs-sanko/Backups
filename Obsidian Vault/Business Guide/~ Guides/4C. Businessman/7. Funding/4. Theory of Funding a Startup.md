- Information asymmetries Due to
	- Lacking a track record
	- Intangible assets
	- Unpredicatble feasibility of new technologu
	- Fear of being imitated
- **information asymmetries lead investors to ask for a premium to cover the risk of investing in a low quality startup, and makes external capital expensive.**
- Investors are not able to distinguish between good and bad companies. Therefore investors ask for a premium. (Lemon premium) This premium is acceptable to bad companies since they are selling worse things anyways but unacceptable to good companies and results in the good companies leaving the market.
