Lump Sum Numbers
- NPV: Net positive value. Real money made over project lifetime. Bu negatifse paranı cost of equity hesapladığın araca yatırman daha mantıklı. Bu araç hiçbirşey olmasa eurobond oluyor.


Ratios
- ==Equity IRR (EIRR)==: The rated form of NPV. How good the real return is over the complete project life compared to initial investment. (Magnitude of return) Reflects investor returns Compare this to cost of equity or WACC.
- Cost of equity is a measure of opportunity cost if the money was kept in eurobonds. (Or another number if you have another business that is more profitable than eurobonds you can put the same money into.)
- Project IRR: Pure profitability of the operating business itself independent of how it was financed. Excludes debt service. Reflects asset profitability.
- ==THE IRR DOESNT EXIST IF THE PROJECT IS TOO PROFITABLE FROM THE GETGO==

Rate of Return in Years
- WART: Weighted Average Return Time: Whiich year the center of gravity of the returns are. Lower means higher returns in a shorter time. Best complimentary to WART. -> Time for earliness of returns
- ==Discounted Payback Period (DPP)== -> When the project becomes self sufficient (when it becomes a cash engine) (Speed of return). Use this instead of the bakkal hesabı Yatırım+Interest / EBITDA. -> Time for returning equity This is a better version of the payback period which takes cumulative cash flow only.
- NPV/CAPEX or NPV/MW or NPV of project / NPV of cepten çıkaracağın paralar

WACC: Weighted average of the equity with the cost ofeauyity and debt with the cost of debt. NPV de iskonto ornaı olarak kullanılır. Senin sermayenin maliyetini karşılayabilmek için IRR en az WACC kadar olmalıdır. 
WACC IRR'dan küçük olsa da NPV negatif olabilir bunların ikişsine de bakılmalıdır hem IRR WACC'ı geçmeli hem de NPV pozitif olmalıdır. NPV negatifse parayı cost of equity kaynağında değerlendirmek daha mantıklıdır.

Leverage (Debt to Equity): But it also swings easier to the negative since it is leveraged. (Debt service overwhelms you more easily if IRR is not what you expected)
